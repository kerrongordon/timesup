Welcome to Time's Up

The Time's Up app was made to help studens with time management for school.

New Schedule

Adding New Schedule is very easy with one simple tap of the blue button add a title description date and time and a custom color.

Notifications

Get Notify once a schedule is ready you can tap on the notification to go right to the schedule to get more information.

Search

You can Search for all you schedules fast and easy and swipe left to delete them or tap to open a schedule.
